The Brain

Purpose: Understanding the structure and organization of the nervous system.

Overview of the nervous system

Summary and orientation. Use the interactive brain on the homepage to jump to specific regions and return here for in-depth articles.

  • Central nervous system — brain and spinal cord.
  • Peripheral nervous system — nerves and ganglia outside the CNS.
  • Autonomic nervous system — sympathetic and parasympathetic branches.

Cortical lobes

  • Frontal lobe — executive functions, planning, motor control.
  • Parietal lobe — somatosensory processing and spatial reasoning.
  • Temporal lobe — auditory processing, memory, language.
  • Occipital lobe — vision.

Brain regions

  • Hippocampus — memory formation.
  • Amygdala — emotion and fear processing.
  • Prefrontal cortex — higher cognition and planning.
  • Basal ganglia — motor control and habit learning.
  • Thalamus — sensory relay.
  • Hypothalamus — homeostasis and endocrine control.
  • Brainstem — vital functions and arousal.
  • Cerebellum — coordination and motor learning.
