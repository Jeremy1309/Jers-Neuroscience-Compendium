Neuroscience Concepts & Research

Flexible section for deeper topics and current research.

Synaptic Plasticity

Synaptic plasticity refers to the ability of synapses to change in strength over time in response to activity and experience. It is a core mechanism supporting learning and memory.

Learning & Memory

Memory depends on coordinated activity across cortical and subcortical circuits, especially in the hippocampus and related networks. Repeated activation and reinforcement shape durable memory traces.

Neurodevelopment

Neurodevelopment studies how the nervous system forms, matures, and adapts from early embryonic stages through adolescence and adulthood.

Consciousness

Consciousness involves the integrated activity of distributed neural systems and remains one of the central questions in neuroscience.
